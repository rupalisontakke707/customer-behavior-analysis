# 📊 Customer Behavior Analysis

## 🎯 Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze customer purchasing behavior and extract meaningful insights to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **Python** (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- **MySQL** (Data querying & analysis)
- **Power BI** (Interactive dashboard)
- **Jupyter Notebook**

---

## 📊 Project Workflow
1. Data Collection & Loading  
2. Data Cleaning & Preprocessing  
3. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)  
4. SQL-Based Analysis  
5. Customer Segmentation (RFM Analysis)  
6. Data Visualization using Power BI  

---

## 📈 Analysis Performed
- Revenue analysis by category, gender, and age group  
- Customer segmentation (New, Returning, Loyal)  
- Subscription impact on spending behavior  
- Discount analysis and its effect on purchases  
- Product performance analysis  
- RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) Analysis  

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- Customers with subscriptions tend to spend more than non-subscribed customers  
- A small group of high-value customers contributes significantly to total revenue  
- Discounts positively influence customer purchasing behavior  
- Certain product categories dominate overall sales performance
